Artist Schedule

Reserve

Hard rock lives here! It's loud and thriving at Booze 'N' Bands. We bring the best of both worlds: the legends that built the genre and the new artists keeping it alive.

Reserve

April 24th - 26th: Tribute Week

Thursday April 24th

Riff Raiders (AC/DC Tribute)

High-voltage rock ‘n’ roll that hits. Riff Raiders bring the raw energy, dirty riffs, and fist-pumping anthems of AC/DC to life, making sure you feel every electrifying note. Get ready to shake the place all night long!

Friday April 25th

Blackened Souls (Metallica Tribute)

Heavy, fast, and loud, just how it should be. Blackened Souls unleashes the fury of Metallica’s greatest hits, from the classics to the bone-crushing deep cuts. No compromises, no holding back, just pure metal mayhem.

Saturday April 26th

Revolver Saints (Guns 'N' Roses Tribute)

Welcome to the jungle, baby! Revolver Saints deliver the attitude, swagger, and searing guitar solos that made Guns N’ Roses legendary. Every note, every scream, every damn ounce of chaos—just like the real deal.

May 1st - 3rd: Upcomers Week

Thursday May 1st

Voltage

Raw, gritty, and loud as hell. Voltage fuses old-school hard rock with modern edge, delivering bone-crushing riffs and anthems built for fist-pumping mayhem. If you like your rock dirty and unapologetic, this is your band.

Friday May 2nd

Rustborn

Rustborn take the reckless spirit of rock and crank it to eleven. Gritty melodies, powerhouse choruses, and pure adrenaline-fueled anthems make them a band that demands to be heard—loud and live.

Saturday May 3rd

Neon Reckless

Big hooks, bold riffs, and anthems that hit hard. Neon Reckless blends the raw energy of rock with the infectious melodies of pop. Their sound is fierce, electrifying, and built for singing along at the top of your lungs.

May 8- 10: Metal Week

Thursday May 8th

Amplify

Crushing riffs, relentless drums, and guttural vocals. Amplify drags you into the abyss with their brutal yet hypnotic sound. They forge metal that is both sinister and anthemic, built for those who crave most intense sonic experience.

Friday May 9th

Iron Revenant (Iron Maiden Tribute)

Iron Revenant keeps the Maiden legacy alive. With note-for-note precision and pure passion, they deliver a high-voltage tribute that takes fans straight back to the golden era of heavy metal.
