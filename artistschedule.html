Artist Schedule

April 24th - 26th: Tribute Week

Thursday April 24th

Riff Raiders (AC/DC Tribute)

High-voltage rock ‘n’ roll that hits. Riff Raiders bring the raw energy, the riffs, and the powerhouse tracks of AC/DC to life, making sure you feel every electrifying note. Get ready to shake the place all night long!

Friday April 25th

Blackened Souls (Metallica Tribute)

Heavy, fast, and loud, just how it should be. Blackened Souls unleashes the fury of Metallica’s greatest hits, from the classics to the hard-hitting, hidden treasures. No holding back, just pure metal rock.

Saturday April 26th

Revolver Saints (Guns 'N' Roses Tribute)

Step into the wild! Revolver Saints delivers the attitude, bold confidence, riffs and searing guitar solos that made Guns 'N' Roses legendary. Every riff, every shout, every ounce of chaos, just like the real deal.

May 1st - 3rd: Upcomers Week

Thursday May 1st

Voltage

Raw, gritty, and loud. Voltage fuses old-school hard rock with modern edge, delivering mind-blowing riffs and songs designed for wild fist-pumping chaos. If you like your rock dirty and unapologetic, this is your band.

Friday May 2nd

Rustborn

Rustborn takes the reckless spirit of rock and cranks it up. Gritty melodies, powerhouse lyrics, and pure adrenaline-fueled songs make them a band that demands to be heard. They are loud and unstoppable!

Saturday May 3rd

Neon Reckless

Big hooks, bold riffs, and songs that hit hard. Neon Reckless blends the raw energy of rock with the irresistable melodies of pop. Their sound is fierce, electrifying, and built for singing along at the top of your lungs.

May 8th- 10th: Metal Week

Thursday May 8th

Amplify

Crushing riffs, relentless drums, and loud vocals. Amplify drags you into the depths with their brutal yet hypnotic sound. They forge metal that is both dark and epic, built for those who crave most intense sonic experience.

Friday May 9th

Iron Revenant (Iron Maiden Tribute)

Iron Revenant keeps the Maiden legacy alive. With flawless precision and pure passion, they deliver an electrifying tribute that takes you straight back to the golden era of heavy metal.

Saturday May 10th

Legendary

Fierce riffs, lightning speed, and loud breakdowns. Warborn Legion delivers trash in its purest form. Combining old-school energy with modern intensity, they ignite mosh pits and shake venues to the core.
